Our Story

Where the River Meets the Smoke

Just 1km from Victoria Falls, The Smokehouse has become the perfect partner to River Brewing Co — a place where fire-grilled food meets handcrafted beer on tap. Our bold dishes, from crocodile souvlaki to classic beef burgers, pair seamlessly with our brews.

By day, it's the ideal spot to relax after exploring the Falls. By night, the vibe shifts — DJs take to the decks, live bands light up the stage, and the Smokehouse comes alive.

Craft Beer

Locally brewed River beers on tap — from crisp lagers to bold IPAs — crafted just steps from where you sit. Every pint tells the story of Victoria Falls.

Smokehouse Kitchen

Fire-grilled perfection. Slow-smoked ribs, peri-peri chicken, signature burgers, and crocodile souvlaki — all kissed by flame and crafted with passion.

Live Events

From acoustic sunsets to DJ-driven nights, our stage hosts Zimbabwe's finest talent. Every visit brings a new beat, a new memory, a new story.
